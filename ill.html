Digital Illustration

Audience Year 7-10
Level GCSE
Discipline ICT, Design
Topics Design, Digital art, Data encoding

Presentations for creating digital art.

Pop Art

This presentation crosses over into the world of Pop Art. The concept is simple: choose a photograph of yourself or someone famous, outline the subject and use the fill function to create a Pop Art inspired image.

Barcodes

Barcodes are cool! They don't need to be boring and more and more companies are using barcodes as an opportunity for design. This lesson shows you how you can make them pop.

Barcodes PDF

Supplement to the above lesson, this PDF contains detailed, step-by-step instructions for creating cool-looking barcodes in Photoshop. Check that Photoshop has the barcode font installed before delivering.

Barcode treasure hunt

This PDF can be used as a starter or as part of the above barcode lesson. It contains clues in barcode form. A scanner connected to a computer is required with a text editor open. When each barcode is scanned, the scanner prints text in the text editor providing a clue. A mobile app barcode scanner can also be used.
